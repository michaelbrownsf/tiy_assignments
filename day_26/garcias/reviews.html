Vicki A. -

awesome little Resturant. Staff is always friendly and food is alway soooooo good!!! Not on the menu? Ask.. They will make it if they can. This place is a gem... Best kept secret!!!

Christina W. -

My husband and I tried this place out for dinner. The restaurant was clean and festive. We got complimentary salsa and chips which was good. The chips were warm and the salsa was mild which is more appealing to the masses.

Jim O. -

I know I've mentioned the great rice & beans before but now I can tell you about there best entree. Spinach Enchiladas. They were to die for! And I realize I've failed to mention the wait staff.

Today we had Maureen who is awesome, but Ana is great also. My friend Ray says all the staff is great, but these two stand out!

K M. -

Garcia's is by far my favorite Mexican place within a 20 mile radius (even though they have like 5 other locations within that radius). I freaking LOVE their fajitas nortenas. Onions, tomatoes, and fresh jalapeno slices with dat chicken is amazing. Corn tortillas, salsa, charro beans, and guacamole - ALL GREAT.

Joseph V. -
